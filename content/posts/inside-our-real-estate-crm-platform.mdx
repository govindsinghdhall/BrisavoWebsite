## One operating system for real estate teams

Real estate operations break when tools fragment. Leads live in a spreadsheet. Site visits live in chat. Campaigns live in another vendor. Reporting never matches reality.

Our Real Estate CRM is designed as a single system of record for:

- Contacts and lead pipelines
- Properties and bookings
- Site visit scheduling
- Roles and organization settings
- WhatsApp marketing campaigns
- Unified reporting

## Multi-tenant by design

Every organization gets isolated configuration and data boundaries, with JWT authentication and role-based permissions for secure team access.

That foundation lets agencies and developers scale from a small sales pod to multi-city operations without rewriting the platform.

## Messaging where deals actually happen

WhatsApp is not a side channel in many markets — it is the channel.

The CRM connects outreach to CRM state so teams can:

- Send template or custom messages
- Run campaigns from stored audiences
- Keep conversation context next to lead and property records

## Product principles we refuse to compromise

1. **Operational clarity** over feature sprawl
2. **Security defaults** over optional hardening
3. **Mobile-ready workflows** for field teams
4. **Reporting that operators trust**

## What’s next

We continue expanding automation and AI assistance on top of this core — scoring, routing, and assistant workflows that reduce manual follow-up without removing human judgment.
